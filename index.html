About Us

About Us

DICEGAME is a decentralized Graphene-based platform that builds a fair and transaprent ground for P2P games. We retrieve user’s trust by wrapping all the game logic into the smart-contracts based on the blockchain. Graphene technology integrated into the DICEGAME allows to have low fees and high speed transactions. The platform provides tha playground for the P2P game with up 50% of winning odds. We decentralize everything in the DICEGAME platform: only white box algorithms, clear and user-friendly conditions of the games, fast and transparent transactions, no limitations on deposits and withdrawals.

Features

Say goodbye to Random Number Generator

Users play with each other, but not against the platform. DICEGAME is just the initiator and organizator of a game.

No special skills required to increase winning odds

Dice roll is the only task to be solved which does not require any particular knowledge or strategy. Just do the rolling and blockchain will take care of all other calculations and estimations.

High level of security

Do you have a way to change the data in more than 50K ledgers when there are 100K of them in the P2P network? Definetely, the answer is "no". But, in case there is a minor chance to do this, then does it really make sense at all? Thus, with the help of the blockchain we make it evident that the architecture of decentralized ledgers makes it almost impossible to lose funds or private information to cybercriminals.

How it Work

The technical implementation provides compliance with the principles and values that are common for all games based on the DICEGAME platform. It offers such advantages as transparency and easy check of results. All key algorithms related to the game mechanisms and winner determination are implemented by the means of the blockchain technology. Blockchain also allows to have reliable and secure transactions for bet accepting, winners determination and awarding, fees charging, etc.

There are two main factors that determine the selection of the algorithm: no interest to any fraud, simplicity in understanding of process and verification of results. To get more information about inner working details of the DICEGAME platform, please review the video "How it works?"

Dice Tokens

The Dice token is a gaming chip used in the games of the DICEGAME platform that allows players not to lose touch with reality of the game with floding cash. We can use Dice in a game inside our platform, exchange to any other cryptocurrency using the integrated Dice Wallet, cash it out.

Key benefits of the Dice tokens:

  • Low fee using the Dice token in a game (1%);
  • High speed of transactions;
  • 100% of transparent transactions;
  • Possibility to convert the Dice token into any cryptocurrency using the Dice Wallet integrated into our platform;
  • High volume of the Dice token usage as a result of constant marketing campaigns all over the world;

Bonus Tournaments

The concept of the project is to conduct regular bonus tournaments. Every new tournament or one-to-one game continuously contributes to the prize fund of the BT. The half of the fee charged by the platform goes to the common prize fund of the BT which is distributed between all bonus tournaments. The first bonus tournament on the DICEGAME platform will have the prize fund of 5% from the all Dice tokens issued. There are no any investments required to participate in this BT.

Terms of participation in the bonus tournaments (BT):

  • A participant can exchange nominal chips for the BT ticket (on daily basis when joining the platform it gives nominal chips, so that the player has the possibility to use them in a game and earn a fair number of additional chips to make the exchange for the BT ticket).
  • There is a chance to gain the ticket in the freeroll tournament (on regular basis the platform generates tournaments with the BT tickets as the prize find).
  • The BT ticket can be provided as a bonus after registration in the platform, or as a present for any holiday.
  • The BT ticket can provided as the award from the platform as a result of participation in any quests.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the DICEGAME?

DICEGAME is a decentralized Graphene-based platform that builds a fair and transaprent ground for P2P game.

What are the main features of the DICEGAME platform that make it unique?

Absence of centralized random number generator, no special skills required to increase winning odds, high level of security, high transaction speeds, low transaction fees, face-to-face games in the peer-to-peer network, deposits and withdrawals at user's disposal, bonus tournaments.

Why Graphene is selected as the base technology?

The decentralized platform DICEGAME is based on the Graphene blockchain. The powerful features of the Graphene help to achive our goals of low transaction fees and high speeds. Firstly, in stress tests the Graphene technology has achieved 10,000-100,000 transactions per second. Secondly, parallelization is used to scale the network, likely up to millions of transactions per second. Thirdly, the asynchronous communications and separate authentication from execution is implemented to achieve speedups, and because it will have no transaction fees, counting operations are not required.

What is the Bonus tournament?

The half of this fee goes to the common prize fund of the BT, which is distributed between all bonus tournaments with the corresponding amount of the prize mentioned in the table below. Thus, prize funds will have continuous growth.
